About

Practical AI guides for everyday work.

Easy ChatGPT Guide helps busy people use AI tools to finish real tasks, such as writing emails, cleaning spreadsheets, summarizing meetings, preparing sales follow-ups, and organizing weekly work.

The site focuses on step-by-step workflows instead of broad AI theory. Each guide is written to help readers understand what to prepare, what to ask, and how to check the final result.

Who this site is for

  • Professionals who want to save time on daily office work.
  • Small business owners who need simple AI workflows.
  • Freelancers who handle client communication and project updates.
  • Creators, teachers, and operators who want useful prompts without technical language.
