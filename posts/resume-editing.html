How to Use ChatGPT to Edit Your Resume

May 13, 2026 • Job search workflow • 7 min read

ChatGPT can help improve a resume, but it should not make up achievements. The best use is rewriting rough bullets, matching language to a job description, and making impact easier to see.

What to prepare

  • Your current resume bullet.
  • The job description.
  • Real numbers, tools, results, and responsibilities.
  • Any words you do not want to use.

Copy this prompt

Improve these resume bullets without inventing facts. Target role: [role] Job description: [paste relevant parts] My current bullets: [paste bullets] Real facts I can support: [tools, numbers, results, responsibilities] Return: 1. Stronger resume bullets 2. A plain-English explanation of what changed 3. Any missing metrics I should try to add 4. Words that sound too generic or AI-written

Common mistake

Do not use polished bullets if you cannot explain them in an interview. Ask ChatGPT to keep your experience accurate and natural.
