How to Summarize a Report with ChatGPT

May 13, 2026 • Office workflow • 6 min read

ChatGPT can help summarize long reports, but you should ask for a specific kind of summary. A manager may need decisions and risks, while a team may need next actions.

Copy this prompt

Summarize this report for a business reader. Audience: [executive, manager, team, client] Purpose: [why they need the summary] Report text or notes: [paste content] Return: 1. Executive summary under 120 words 2. Key findings 3. Risks or concerns 4. Decisions needed 5. Action items 6. Questions the report does not answer

Common mistake

Do not accept a summary that only shortens the text. Ask ChatGPT to separate findings, risks, decisions, and next actions.
