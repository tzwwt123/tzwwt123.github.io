How to Improve Your LinkedIn Profile with ChatGPT

May 13, 2026 • Career workflow • 7 min read

LinkedIn profile writing is difficult because it needs to sound specific, confident, and still like you. ChatGPT can help turn rough career notes into better profile copy, but you need to give it real examples.

What to prepare

  • Your target role or audience.
  • Current headline and About section.
  • Top skills and achievements.
  • Industries or keywords you want to be found for.

Copy this prompt

Improve my LinkedIn profile copy. Target audience: [recruiters, clients, hiring managers, industry peers] Target role or positioning: [role] Current headline: [headline] Current About section: [about section] Real achievements and skills: [facts] Return: 1. 5 headline options 2. A revised About section under 220 words 3. 5 experience bullet ideas 4. Keywords I should naturally include 5. Phrases that sound too generic

Common mistake

Do not let ChatGPT turn your profile into a buzzword list. Specific proof is stronger than “results-driven professional.”
