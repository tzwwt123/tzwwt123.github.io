How to Write a Client Proposal with ChatGPT

May 13, 2026 • Freelance workflow • 7 min read

A proposal should help a client decide. ChatGPT can help organize your thinking, but it should not invent prices, guarantees, or deliverables. Use it to make the proposal clearer and more persuasive.

What to prepare

  • Client problem and desired outcome.
  • Scope of work.
  • Deliverables.
  • Timeline and milestones.
  • Price or pricing range, if ready.
  • What is not included.

Copy this prompt

Draft a client proposal from these notes. Client: [client type] Problem: [problem] Desired outcome: [outcome] Scope: [scope] Deliverables: [deliverables] Timeline: [timeline] Not included: [exclusions] Return: 1. Proposal summary 2. Project scope 3. Deliverables 4. Timeline 5. Assumptions and exclusions 6. Next step email

Common mistake

Do not let ChatGPT make your proposal too long. After the first draft, ask it to remove vague promises and shorten the document for a busy client.
