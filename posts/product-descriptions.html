How to Write Product Descriptions with ChatGPT

May 13, 2026 • Ecommerce workflow • 6 min read

Product descriptions should be specific, accurate, and easy to scan. ChatGPT can help turn product facts into benefits, but you need to provide details instead of asking for generic sales copy.

Copy this prompt

Write product descriptions using only these facts. Product: [product] Audience: [buyer] Features: [features] Benefits: [benefits] Materials/specs: [details] Objections or questions: [concerns] Return: 1. Short product description 2. Longer product description 3. 5 benefit bullets 4. SEO title and meta description 5. Claims I should verify before publishing

Common mistake

Do not ask ChatGPT to make a weak product sound premium. Better copy comes from better facts, proof, and clear benefits.
