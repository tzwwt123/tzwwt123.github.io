How to Write a Job Description with ChatGPT

May 13, 2026 • Hiring workflow • 7 min read

A job description should help the right person understand the role and help you evaluate candidates. ChatGPT can organize responsibilities, requirements, and success measures from your rough notes.

Copy this prompt

Draft a job description. Role title: [title] Company type: [company] Why we are hiring: [reason] Main responsibilities: [responsibilities] Required skills: [skills] Nice-to-have skills: [optional] Success in 90 days: [outcomes] Return: 1. Job summary 2. Responsibilities 3. Required qualifications 4. Nice-to-have qualifications 5. 90-day success measures 6. Interview questions based on the role

Common mistake

Do not copy a generic job description. Add the real reason the role exists and what success looks like.
