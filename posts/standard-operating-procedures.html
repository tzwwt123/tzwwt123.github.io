How to Create SOPs with ChatGPT

May 13, 2026 • Business workflow • 7 min read

Small businesses often keep processes in someone's head. ChatGPT can help turn rough notes into a standard operating procedure, but the person who knows the process must review every step.

What to prepare

  • The process name and purpose.
  • Who does the work.
  • Inputs, tools, and files needed.
  • Steps in rough order.
  • Common mistakes or quality checks.

Copy this prompt

Turn these process notes into an SOP. Process name: [name] Purpose: [purpose] Who performs it: [role] Tools or files needed: [tools] Rough steps: [paste steps] Quality checks: [checks] Return: 1. SOP overview 2. Step-by-step process 3. Checklist 4. Common mistakes 5. Questions I need to answer before this is complete

Common mistake

Do not publish an SOP without testing it. Give the draft to someone and see if they can complete the process from the instructions.
