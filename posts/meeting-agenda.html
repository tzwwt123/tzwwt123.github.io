How to Create a Meeting Agenda with ChatGPT

May 14, 2026 • Meeting workflow • 6 min read

A meeting agenda should make the meeting easier to run. ChatGPT can help when you give it the purpose, people, decisions, and time limit.

Best use cases

  • Preparing a client call.
  • Turning a vague meeting invite into a real agenda.
  • Planning a project kickoff.
  • Separating discussion topics from decisions.
  • Creating follow-up questions before the meeting starts.

Copy this prompt

Create a focused meeting agenda. Meeting purpose: [why we are meeting] Attendees: [roles or names] Time available: [30 minutes, 45 minutes, 60 minutes] Background: [brief context] Decisions needed: [list decisions] Return: 1. Meeting objective 2. Timed agenda 3. Questions to ask 4. Decisions to confirm 5. Follow-up email template

Common mistake

Do not create an agenda that is just a topic list. A useful agenda includes the outcome each section should produce.
