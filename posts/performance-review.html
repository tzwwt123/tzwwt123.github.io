How to Use ChatGPT for Performance Review Comments

May 13, 2026 • Workplace workflow • 6 min read

Performance reviews are easier when your notes are specific. ChatGPT can turn rough accomplishments into clearer comments, but it should not invent results or soften important feedback too much.

Copy this prompt

Help me write performance review comments. Review type: [self-review / manager feedback] Role: [role] Achievements: [real examples] Challenges: [real examples] Metrics or outcomes: [numbers/results] Development goals: [goals] Tone: Specific, fair, professional, and not exaggerated. Return: 1. Achievement summary 2. Improvement areas 3. Development goals 4. A shorter version 5. Vague phrases I should replace

Common mistake

A review that says “strong communication” is weaker than one that explains when communication helped a project. Give ChatGPT examples, not just traits.
