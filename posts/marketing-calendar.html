How to Build a Marketing Calendar with ChatGPT

May 13, 2026 • Marketing workflow • 7 min read

A marketing calendar is easier to create when ChatGPT knows your business, audience, offer, and channels. The best result is not a list of random post ideas. It is a simple plan with themes, deadlines, and calls to action.

What to prepare

  • Your product or service.
  • Your target audience.
  • Main offer or promotion.
  • Channels: blog, email, TikTok, LinkedIn, Instagram, local SEO.
  • How often you can realistically publish.

Copy this prompt

Create a 4-week marketing calendar. Business: [business] Audience: [audience] Offer: [offer] Channels: [channels] Publishing capacity: [how many posts/emails per week] Return: 1. Weekly theme 2. Content ideas by channel 3. Simple call to action 4. Repurposing ideas 5. One metric to track each week

Common mistake

Do not accept 30 ideas if you can only publish 4. Ask ChatGPT to build around your actual capacity.
