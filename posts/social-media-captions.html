ChatGPT Prompts for Social Media Captions

May 13, 2026 • Marketing workflow • 6 min read

Good captions are not just clever lines. They need a clear audience, a reason to care, and a next step. ChatGPT can help create variations, but it needs your offer and context.

Copy this prompt

Write social media captions for this post. Platform: [Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Facebook, X] Audience: [audience] Post topic: [topic] Offer or goal: [goal] Tone: [friendly, expert, playful, direct] Return: 1. 5 caption options 2. 3 short hooks 3. 3 calls to action 4. A less salesy version 5. Hashtag ideas if useful

Common mistake

Do not ask for captions before deciding the point of the post. A caption for education is different from a caption for a sale or testimonial.
