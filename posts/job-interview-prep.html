How to Use ChatGPT for Job Interview Prep

May 13, 2026 • Job search workflow • 7 min read

Interview prep works well with ChatGPT when you use it as a practice partner. It can generate likely questions, help structure examples, and point out vague answers before the interview.

Copy this prompt

Help me prepare for a job interview. Role: [role] Job description: [paste job description] My background: [summary] Projects or achievements I can discuss: [examples] Return: 1. 10 likely interview questions 2. 5 behavioral questions 3. STAR answer outlines using my real examples 4. Questions I should ask the interviewer 5. Weak spots I should prepare for

Practice follow-up

After you write answers, ask ChatGPT to play the interviewer and challenge unclear points. This helps you prepare for follow-up questions instead of memorizing a script.
