How to Write a Cover Letter with ChatGPT

May 13, 2026 • Job search workflow • 6 min read

ChatGPT can make cover letters less painful, but generic letters are easy to spot. The strongest workflow is to give it the job description, your real experience, and a few specific reasons you want the role.

Copy this prompt

Draft a cover letter without inventing facts. Job title: [title] Company: [company] Job description: [paste relevant parts] My relevant experience: [real examples] Why I want this role: [reason] Tone: Clear, specific, confident, not overly formal. Return: 1. Cover letter under 300 words 2. A shorter email version 3. 3 lines that sound generic and should be improved

Common mistake

Do not let the letter become a repeat of your resume. Ask ChatGPT to connect your experience to the employer's needs.
