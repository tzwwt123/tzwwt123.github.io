ChatGPT for Real Estate Listing Descriptions

May 13, 2026 • Real estate workflow • 6 min read

Real estate listings need to sound appealing without becoming exaggerated or inaccurate. ChatGPT can help turn property facts into a readable description, but the facts must come from you.

What to prepare

  • Property type, location, beds, baths, square footage.
  • Best features and recent updates.
  • Nearby amenities.
  • Required legal or brokerage language.
  • Words or claims to avoid.

Copy this prompt

Write a real estate listing description using only these facts. Property facts: [paste facts] Best features: [features] Neighborhood details: [details] Audience: [first-time buyers, families, investors, renters] Restrictions: Do not exaggerate, do not invent amenities, and avoid fair housing risk language. Return: 1. Listing description under 180 words 2. Short version under 80 words 3. 5 headline options 4. A checklist of facts I should verify

Common mistake

Do not ask ChatGPT to “make it luxury” if the property facts do not support that. Strong listing copy is specific, not inflated.
